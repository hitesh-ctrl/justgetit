GET | MIT Campus Thrift Marketplace
GET is a hyper-local, peer-to-peer thrift platform exclusively designed for the students of Madras Institute of Technology (MIT), Chromepet. It facilitates the buying, selling, and requesting of pre-owned items within the campus ecosystem.
